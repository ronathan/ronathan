WE'RE
PLANTING
THE SEEDS.

We're building homes, not houses. Neighbourhoods, not developments. With an eye to the future and a foot in the past, we're creating modern communities designed to stand the test of time. Communities where your family can put down roots, and love living for years to come.

We are Cortel. And we care about the legacy we leave behind.

  • OUR ROOTS
    RUN DEEP.

    We are the Cortel Group. We're a family-run collective of diverse, dynamic and successful businesses that build commercial and residential communities throughout Toronto, its neighbouring cities, and even across the province.

    WHAT WE BELIEVE

  • WHAT WE BELIEVE

    We don't just build houses. We create what we call complete communities: outstanding, livable neighbourhoods with the recreation, green space, shopping, services and transportation we all need. Somewhere you and your family can easily and happily grow, now and into the future.

    We are responsible to the environment. That's why we use the latest technology, along with sustainable materials and practices, to help us build innovative communities that respect nature. Partners like the Lake Simcoe Region Conservation Authority (LSRCA), Greenland Group of Companies and the University of Guelph help us leave as little a footprint as possible. Together, we work towards flood reduction and nutrient load elimination from uncontrolled headwater areas, creek rehabilitation and pond management. We're also involved in the preservation of natural heritage sites. While we look towards the future, it's important to us that we respect our history.

    A collection of buildings doesn't make a community - it's the people that help it grow that really matter. That's why we give to local charities and organizations that make it their job to give back.

    A promise is a promise. We say what we mean, and we mean what we say. You can take our word for it, every time.

    Our dedication to these beliefs has earned us first-rate reputation in the industry, and among the people who live in our communities.

    OUR ROOTS RUN DEEP
    OUR LOGO

  • OUR LOGO

    We chose an iconic Canadian symbol - the humble maple key - because it represents to what's most important to us. The seed symbolizes the beginning stages of development: the vision and planning for a new community. The circle stands for the realization of that vision.

    Our logo speaks to the promise of new life. It speaks to a hopeful beginning. And it speaks to the way something small can grow into something powerful and enduring.

  • GIVING IS
    GROWING.

    We're all about creating better communities - and that means looking beyond the homes we build. We're honoured to support the charities and organizations that work tirelessly to make our communities better places to live, work and play.

    Nothing brings us greater pride than to know those in need benefit from our help. Causes close to our heart include youth education, healthcare, and First Nations initiatives like University Youth Foundation, St. Michael's Foundation, the Art Gallery of Ontario and Ducks Unlimited.
